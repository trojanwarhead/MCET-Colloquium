ACME

PAPER PRESENTATION


SUBMIT YOUR PAPER REGISTER

DESCRIPTION

A platform to display your know- how on various topics in various fields of engineering and presentation skills. The aim of this event is to stimulate the quest for knowledge by the way of testing the exploratory, analatycal and the presentation skills of the participants. It provides students a platform to voice their views and an opportunity to communicate and compete with the best. It networks the young engineers from various academic institutions for better knowledge flow and Exchange.

RULES

  • A maximum of three authors are allowed per paper.
  • Presentation for PG participants will be conducted on 7th at the same venue listed in the schedule.
  • Postgraduate participants are allowed but are not eligible for cash prize. However a prize will be given to the best paper among the PG papers.
  • The participants have to mail the abstract of the paper to mcetcolloquium01@gmail.com on or before the 23th of Feburary 2018.
  • The participants will recieve intimation about Acceptance/Rejection on or before 26th of Feburary 2018.
  • The participants have to mail the DD in favour of Director, MCET payable at Canara Bank.
  • The participants who are short-listed based on abstract selection must send their full paper (limited to 6 pages) on or before 6th of March 2017.
  • Participants should report at the venue at-least 30 minutes before the commencement of the event.
  • Participants should bring three hard copies of the paper along with their presentation done in Microsoft Powerpoint in a CD/USB.
  • Participants will be provided a maximum presentation time of 10 minutes followed by a 3 minutes questionnaire session.
  • LCD projector will be provided.
  • The decision of the judges will be final and binding in case of any disputes.
  • All updates regarding the event will be posted in the colloquium official site and our facebook page. It is the responsibility of participants to visit the site or page to get notified. No claim with this regard will be entertained.
