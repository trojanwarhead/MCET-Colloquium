Colloquium 18



We are back with it's annual Technical Festival the Colloquium.Colloquium'18 will be conducted on 16th & 17th of March,2018 .The aim of Colloquium is to identify and encourage creative talents of budding Engineers and technologists throughout the country.This two day national level technical symposium conducted every year for the undergraduate students of Biotechnology, Computer Science, Information Technology ,Civil Engineering ,Electrical and Electronics,Electronics & Communication and Mechanical Engineering and MCA provides a platform for the students of the engineering fraternity for exchange of their ideas and information to keep abreast of the current developments. The overall objective of this mega event is to bring together the expertise and showcase the talents of the students in various disciplines. Technical paper presentations for all the above disciplines are conducted in parallel sessions. Other competitions like Hardware debugging, Technical Quiz, Software Debugging, Network Games, Project Contest, Business Idea Presentation, Poster Presentation, Web Design, Engineering Problems, Line Follower (Robots), Documentary Competition,Engineering Drawing,etc to evaluate the general talents of the students are conducted in connection with this two-day programme. Students from leading technical educational institutions and Universities covering a wide geographical designation across the country participate in this programme.

Colloquium'18 offers a cash prizes worth 3 lakhs+ in total. Apart from technical contest, cultural programmes have also been organized. We expect enthusiastic participation from Engineering students all over the country to make this event a grand success.
